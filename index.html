The Artist

About Sweta

Sweta is a globally recognized artist and the winner of ASAS Dubai 2024. Her work blends precision, emotion, and intricate detailing, bringing life to every subject she captures.

She specializes in custom paintings and sketches, creating deeply personal and expressive artworks tailored to each client. Based in Kanpur, India, Sweta is available for commissions worldwide.
